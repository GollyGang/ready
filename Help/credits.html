Pattern competition!

Send us an interesting pattern file. Winners will get their patterns included in the next release of Ready, and have their name mentioned in the credits.

Rules:

  • Patterns should contain good descriptions.
  • Patterns should ideally have small file sizes (e.g. under 100k)
  • Patterns should be freely distributable under our GPL3 license.
  • Competition categories:
    • Best science pattern: showing some phenomenon reported in a published paper.
    • Most original: some new behavior we haven't seen before.
    • Prettiest

Deadline: shortly before the next release.
